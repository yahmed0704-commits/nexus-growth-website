  ---

  ## Website Pages Required

  1. **Home** — Hero, stats, services preview, projects preview, CTA
  2. **About Us** — Company story, values, mission
  3. **Our Team** — Photos, names, titles, bios (President, VP, PM, Coordinator, etc.)
  4. **Current Projects** — Active renovations with status, photos, timelines
  5. **Upcoming Projects** — Future planned properties
  6. **Available Rentals** — Listings with photos, details, inquiry form
  7. **Before & After Gallery** — Kitchen, bathroom, exterior transformations
  8. **Services** — Fix & flip, rental mgmt, construction, investment, development, partnerships
  9. **Investor & Partnership** — Investment opportunities, joint ventures
  10. **Contact Us** — Form, email, phone, map, social links
